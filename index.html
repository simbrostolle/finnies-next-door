Good to know

Do you take reservations?
No — we’re walk-in only. Arrive early on busy weekends and Irish game days.
Is there dancing?
Yes. Finnies is an Irish sports pub that turns into a full-on nightlife spot with DJs, live bands, and dancing.
What’s the dress code?
Casual but put-together. Jerseys and game-day fits welcome, no excessive bagginess or unsafe items.
Are private parties available?
Yes. Reach out by phone or DM for group events, alumni weekends, and celebrations at the Golden Dome bar.
