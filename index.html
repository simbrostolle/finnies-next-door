FAQ

Do you take reservations?
Nope — Finnies is walk-in only. Arrive early on busy weekends and ND home games.
Is there dancing?
Yes. Once the DJs and bands get going, the floor around the Golden Dome becomes the dance floor.
Dress code?
Casual, game-day friendly. Jerseys and ND fits welcome — just no super-baggy or unsafe items.
Private events?
Yes. Reach out by phone or DM to talk through ND weekends, alumni events, and group parties.
